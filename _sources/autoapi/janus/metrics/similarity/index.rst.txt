.. py:function:: similarity_score(target, reference, model_name = 'text-embedding-3-small', distance_metric = 'cosine', **kwargs)

   Computes the similarity score of two strings

   :param target: The target string.
   :param reference: The reference string.
   :param model_name: The name of the embedding model to use.
   :param distance_metric: The distance metric to use. Can be one of:
                           - cosine
                           - euclidean
                           - manhattan
                           - chebyshev
                           - hamming

   :returns: The similarity score of the two strings.
